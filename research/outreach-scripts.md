## Key Principles

1. **Personalize the first line** - mention something specific about them
2. **Lead with their pain** - high drop-offs, high costs, compliance headaches
3. **Quantify your value** - specific numbers (8% drop-off, $0.99, 45 seconds)
4. **Low friction ask** - "Can I send a Loom?" not "Let's schedule a call"
5. **Follow up 3x minimum** - most deals happen on follow-up #2-3
