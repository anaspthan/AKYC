## Week 1: Customer Discovery

### Goals
- 30+ discovery conversations
- Validate pricing ($0.99-$1.49)
- Identify top 3 pain points

### Daily Tasks (Mon-Fri)
- **Morning (1h):** Research 10 prospects, personalize outreach
- **Afternoon (1h):** Send 10 cold messages (Telegram/LinkedIn/Twitter)
- **Evening (30min):** Update tracker, schedule calls

### Targets
- 50 outreach messages sent
- 15-20 responses
- 10 discovery calls completed
- 3-5 hot leads identified

### Key Questions to Validate
1. "What's your current KYC drop-off rate?" (should be 30-60%)
2. "What do you pay per verification?" (should be $2-5)
3. "Would $0.99 with better UX solve a problem?" (looking for YES)

**Outcome:** Clear understanding of customer pain + 3-5 people interested in beta

## Month 4-6: Scale to $30k-$50k

### Key Strategies

**1. Agency Reseller Program**
- 10 agencies @ $999/month = $10k
- They resell at $2-3/verification, keep margin

**2. White-Label Deals**
- 5 customers @ $2,500/month = $12.5k
- Larger companies want their own branding

**3. Enterprise Customers**
- 2-3 customers doing 10k+ verifications/month
- Negotiate volume pricing ($0.80-$1.00)

**4. Hire VA/SDR**
- $300-500/month
- Handle outreach, scheduling, follow-ups
- You focus on demos and closing

### Targets (Month 6)
- 100+ customers (mix of pay-as-you-go, agency, white-label)
- $30k-$50k MRR
- Team: You + 1 VA + 1 part-time support
